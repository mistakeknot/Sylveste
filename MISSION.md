# Sylveste Mission

Build the infrastructure that lets AI agents do complex knowledge work autonomously, safely, and at scale. Software engineering is the proving ground; the primitives generalize. Prove that the bottleneck is plumbing, not intelligence — and that compounding evidence is the path to earned trust.

Two brands, one architecture: Sylveste is the infrastructure platform (kernel, OS, profiler, plugins). Garden Salon is the experience layer where humans and agents think together on shared projects. Meadowsyn bridges them through real-time visualization. The layer boundary is the brand boundary.
