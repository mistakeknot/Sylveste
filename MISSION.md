# Sylveste Mission

Build the infrastructure that lets AI agents do complex knowledge work autonomously, safely, and at scale. The bottleneck is infrastructure, not intelligence — and compounding evidence is the path to earned trust. Ontology to track what's known, governance to gate what's allowed, integration to verify across boundaries, measurement to prove what worked. Software engineering is the proving ground; the primitives generalize.

Two brands, one architecture: Sylveste is the infrastructure platform (kernel, OS, profiler, evidence systems, plugins). Garden Salon is the experience layer where humans and agents think together on shared projects. Meadowsyn bridges them through real-time visualization. The layer boundary is the brand boundary.
