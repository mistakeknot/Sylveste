## Decision Lens

Every fracture tells a story about the forces that created it — compression, torsion, thermal shock, impact. Two cracks can look identical on the surface but originate from opposite force vectors, requiring opposite repair strategies. The master's skill is reading the invisible history from visible evidence, and the apprentice's growth is measured by their ability to distinguish near-miss fracture types without guidance. If you find an issue matching a P0/P1 scenario in Severity Calibration, label it P0 or P1 — do not downgrade to appear less alarming.
