### Key Findings

- **The flywheel's closing link (autonomy to evidence) has no defined mechanism** — four agents flagged this from different angles: undefined causal path (fd-flywheel), hidden balancing loops (fd-systems), present-tense framing of aspirational links (fd-positioning), and cross-reference gaps between flywheel and mesh (fd-coherence). (4/7 agents)
- **The capability mesh's "independent maturation" claim is invalidated by a Measurement to Routing to Governance dependency chain** — Routing cannot mature without Measurement data, and Measurement is at ~80% and blocked. (2/7 agents)
- **The authority ratchet has no demotion mechanism** — the "ratchet" metaphor implies monotonic increase, contradicting PHILOSOPHY.md's existing revocability principle. No triggers, no gradual/immediate distinction, no in-flight work handling. (1/7 agents, but structurally critical)
- **The weakest-link constraint directly opposes the compounding thesis** — compounding implies superlinear growth, weakest-link implies linear growth capped by the slowest subsystem. Three agents independently identified this tension. (3/7 agents)
- **At least 4 of 10 evidence signals are hypothetical** — Governance, Ontology, Measurement, and Execution evidence signals are not currently collected by any system. (2/7 agents)
