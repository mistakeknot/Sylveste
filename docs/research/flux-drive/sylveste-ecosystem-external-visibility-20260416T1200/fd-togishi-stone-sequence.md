# fd-togishi-stone-sequence — Findings

Lens: Honami-lineage togishi. Each surface sits at exactly one stone: arato (coarse), kaisei (foundation), nagura mid-grits, uchigumori (finishing), hadori (edge line). Applying a finishing stone to un-shaped steel pulls visible scratches into the light. The hadori move deliberately dulls adjacent surface so the polished edge shows cleanly.

## Verdict

PHILOSOPHY.md text sits at hazuya-level prose polish. The evidence systems it rests on sit at arato. Finishing grit is being applied to un-shaped steel. Pull the PHILOSOPHY polish back to kaisei honesty, or wait on the text until the steel is shaped.

## Findings

### P0 — PHILOSOPHY.md is uchigumori-polished prose over arato-stage support systems
**Location:** PHILOSOPHY.md twelve claims (target-brief lines 76-89).

The distinctive-claims prose is finished to a high polish. But four of the five cross-cutting evidence systems the claims rest on (Ockham, Interweave, Interop, FluxBench) are M0-M1 — arato stage. Only Interspect is M2+. A technically-serious reader hitting the polished prose pulls the arato scratches of the underlying systems into the light and infers "every claim here is similarly under-constructed."

**Fix:** regress PHILOSOPHY.md prose to kaisei honesty — name which claims have M2+ support, which do not. Example: "Interspect (M2) implements evidence-earns-authority. Ockham, Interweave, Interop, FluxBench are earlier-stage (M0-M1) and are not yet receipts for this claim." Honest kaisei beats dishonest uchigumori.

### P0 — Interspect hamon not hadori'd
**Location:** target-brief §"Architecture" lines 40-63, README architecture table.

Interspect is the one polishable subsystem whose foundation stones are complete. Its hamon (closed-loop artifact — routing decisions calibrated from evidence) would be legible if isolated. But the architecture table lists it next to four M0-M1 systems with equal visual weight. The edge line cannot be seen because the adjacent un-shaped steel reflects light the same way.

**Fix:** hadori the four adjacent systems. Remove Ockham, Interweave, Interop, FluxBench from the public architecture table. Give Interspect its own paragraph, its own diagram, its own page. The hadori is subtraction: what must be dulled so the one finished edge shows.

### P1 — Interchart diagram is hazuya-uniform across 64 plugins
**Location:** mistakeknot.github.io/interchart (target-brief line 114).

The interactive ecosystem diagram shows all 64 plugins at uniform visual weight. Hazuya surface treatment applied evenly produces flatness — no subsystem stands above others. The polish effort is wasted because nothing is legible at a second glance.

**Fix:** either rank by maturity tier (M2+ shown bold, rest dimmed) or remove the diagram from public-facing surfaces until the platform has fewer visible components.

### P2 — Roadmap v1.0 at uchigumori polish, A:L2/B:L1/C:L0 reality at arato
**Location:** `docs/roadmap-v1.md` (target-brief lines 131-132).

The roadmap document is polished. The actual track levels reveal un-shaped steel beneath. Pulling un-shipped v1.0 timeline claims into the light invites "overpromising" assessment.

**Fix:** remove the specific track-level claims from the public roadmap until A:L3 and B:L2 are achieved. State only achieved levels. Future gates stay internal.
