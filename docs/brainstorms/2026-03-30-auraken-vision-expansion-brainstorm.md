# Auraken Vision Expansion Brainstorm

## Trigger

Robinson NTS observation (2026-03-30): best ad/recommendation experiences resemble window shopping in an outdoor mall -- ambient, low-pressure, high-volume filtering where occasional catches feel like great matches. Current chatbot recommendations fail by over-indexing on sparse signals ("you're from Portland -> Patagonia pants").

## Key Decisions

1. **Vision scope**: Life operating system (not just cognitive augmentation). Cognitive core + personal strategist + intelligence amplifier.
2. **Core thesis**: Systems thinking applied to your whole life. The interdisciplinary lens library is the differentiator.
3. **Commerce role**: First-class capability alongside cognitive augmentation.
4. **Anti-dependency model**: Anti-dependency for cognitive augmentation (celebrate independence), ambient/stable for discovery (like a great bookstore).
5. **ProductRecs relationship**: Auraken subsumes ProductRecs. PR becomes the product data backend; Auraken is the consumer-facing AI.
6. **Business model**: Structurally separated affiliate. Rec engine blind to commerce, commerce layer downstream, explicit disclosure, user toggle.
7. **Four domains**: Thinking & decisions, Discovery & recommendations, Learning & growth, Pattern awareness & self-knowledge.
8. **Architecture additions**: Coordination layer (vision-level) + philosophy principles for domain composition and epistemic rigor.

## Flux-Review Findings (16 agents, 4 tracks, 59 findings)

### Two 4/4 convergences:
1. **Use case coordination layer missing** -- every track independently identified this gap
2. **Entity confidence and epistemic rigor** -- profile grows but never challenges itself

### Three P0s:
1. Data governance/GDPR compliance gap
2. Affiliate monetization fiduciary conflict (resolved: structural separation)
3. Lens contraindication signatures missing
